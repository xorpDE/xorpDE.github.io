W. Paleo-Balkan - Pre-Roman western Balkan ancestry. Average of Southern Illyrian, Northern Illyrian, Illyrian, Dardanian, and Bronze Age Western Balkan ancient DNA samples. Highest in Albanians and western Balkan populations.

E. Paleo-Balkan - Pre-Roman eastern Balkan ancestry. Average of Thracian and Dacian ancient DNA samples. Highest in Bulgarians and eastern Balkan populations.

E. Roman Anatolian - Ancestry associated with the Eastern Roman (Byzantine) Empire and Anatolian populations. Highest in Greeks and populations with strong Byzantine influence.

Slavic - Ancestry from medieval Slavic migrations into the Balkans. Derived from Baltic Bronze Age samples representing the Proto-Slavic source population.

Other - Non-local ancestry not captured by the four main sources. Average of South Asian proxy and Turkic samples. Primarily detects Roma and Ottoman-era Turkic admixture. Most Balkan populations score 0%.
